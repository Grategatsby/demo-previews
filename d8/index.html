Common Questions

Auto Repair & Gas Station FAQs for South St Paul, MN

Straight answers about hours, location, payments, and what we can help with.

When is Southview 66 open?

Southview 66 is open Monday through Friday from 7 AM to 6 PM and Saturday from 8 AM to 4 PM. The shop is closed on Sundays. Appointments are recommended to guarantee a spot, though the team can often work in same-day service.

Where is Southview 66 located, and is parking available?

Southview 66 is located at 725 Southview Blvd in South St Paul, Minnesota. The station has a wheelchair accessible entrance, an accessible parking lot, and an accessible restroom, making it an easy stop for drivers of all kinds.

What payment methods does Southview 66 accept?

Southview 66 accepts credit cards, debit cards, and NFC mobile payments such as Apple Pay and Google Pay, so you can pay however works best whether you're filling up at the pump or picking up your car.

Do I need an appointment for auto repair at Southview 66?

Appointments are recommended at Southview 66 to guarantee your vehicle gets seen quickly. Call or text (651) 457-2774 ahead of time — the team has fit in same-day oil changes and quick fixes for drivers with tight schedules before.

What services does Southview 66 offer?

Southview 66 is a full-service auto repair shop and gas station, handling brakes, tune-ups, diagnostics, tires, oil changes, and general repair work, plus full-serve gas at the pump — all in one stop in South St Paul.

Is Southview 66 wheelchair accessible?

Yes. Southview 66 has a wheelchair accessible entrance, an accessible parking lot, and an accessible restroom on site, so customers of all mobility levels can visit comfortably.
