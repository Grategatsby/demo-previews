Routine Maintenance, Handled Right

Oil changes and routine maintenance are the easiest way to keep a car running well for years instead of months. It's not glamorous work, but it's the visit that catches a slipping belt, a low battery, or a fluid leak before any of them turn into a tow. Drivers across South St Paul, West St Paul, and Inver Grove Heights bring their vehicles to us for exactly that reason — a straightforward oil change paired with a real look at the rest of the car, not just a sticker on the windshield telling you when to come back.

What Our Oil Change & Maintenance Service Covers

A maintenance visit at Marie Avenue Service covers more than just the oil:

  • Oil and filter change — fresh oil and a new filter so your engine keeps running clean.
  • Multi-point inspection — a walk-around check of the systems that tend to wear quietly over time.
  • Fluid top-offs and checks — coolant, brake fluid, washer fluid, and transmission fluid.
  • Belts and hoses — a visual check for cracking, fraying, or wear before it becomes a breakdown.
  • Battery check — especially important heading into a Minnesota winter.
  • Tire pressure and tread check — properly inflated tires with healthy tread matter for handling and safety.

It's a single visit that touches the handful of things most likely to cause you trouble later, while your car's already up on the lift.

Signs Your Vehicle Is Due

Some of these are obvious, others are easy to let slide for a few weeks longer than you should:

  • Your oil change reminder light is on, or you're not sure when the last one was.
  • It's been several months, or several thousand miles, since your last oil change.
  • The oil on the dipstick looks dark, gritty, or low.
  • The engine sounds louder or rougher than it used to, especially on startup.
  • It's that time of year — a seasonal checkup before summer road trips or before winter sets in.

If any of that sounds like your car right now, it's a good time to get it in.

What to Expect

Appointments are recommended so we can get your vehicle in and out promptly, though we're always happy to work in a same-day oil change when we can. While your car's in the bay for its oil change, we run through the multi-point check alongside it — fluids, belts and hoses, battery, and tires. If we notice anything else along the way, we'll explain it in plain language, not a wall of codes, and let you decide what to do next. There's no pressure to fix something that doesn't need fixing.

Why Regular Maintenance Matters More in a Minnesota Winter

Cold starts are hard on a vehicle. Engine oil thickens as temperatures drop, which means the right oil viscosity matters more here than it does in a milder climate, and a battery that's been fading all fall can finally give out on the first truly cold morning. A pre-winter check of your battery, belts, and fluids catches those small issues while they're still small — before a cold snap turns them into a car that won't start in your driveway. Add in road salt and the general wear of winter driving around South St Paul and Dakota County, and it's easy to see why regular maintenance visits matter more here than almost anywhere else.
