Good to Know

Massage Therapy FAQs in Woodbury, MN

Answers to what guests ask us most before their first visit.

What are Soothing Asian Health Massage's hours?

Soothing Asian Health Massage is open Monday through Saturday from 10 AM to 8 PM, and Sunday from 11 AM to 8 PM. Appointments are required, so call or text ahead to book your preferred time — same-day openings are often available.

Where is Soothing Asian Health Massage located, and is there parking?

Soothing Asian Health Massage is located at 9900 Valley Creek Rd Ste 130 in Woodbury, Minnesota, inside the Valley Crossing shopping center. The studio has a wheelchair accessible entrance and a wheelchair accessible parking lot, with free shopping-center parking right outside the door.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Appointments are required at Soothing Asian Health Massage. Call or text (651) 239-6002 to book, and ask about same-day availability — guests have been fit in same-day for an aching neck or last-minute tension, so it's always worth asking.

What massage services does Soothing Asian Health Massage offer?

Soothing Asian Health Massage offers relaxation massage, deep tissue massage, and therapeutic pressure-point work for neck, shoulder, and back tension. Pressure is adjusted to your comfort in every session, from a gentle relaxation massage to a firm, deep-tissue session.

What payment methods does Soothing Asian Health Massage accept?

Soothing Asian Health Massage accepts credit cards and debit cards, so you can pay for your session however is most convenient for you at checkout.

Is Soothing Asian Health Massage wheelchair accessible?

Yes. Soothing Asian Health Massage has a wheelchair accessible entrance, a wheelchair accessible parking lot, wheelchair accessible seating, and a wheelchair accessible, gender-neutral restroom, so every guest can visit comfortably.
