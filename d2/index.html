Good to Know

Nail Salon FAQs in Woodbury, MN

Answers to what people ask us most before their first visit.

What are Lexi Nails' hours?

Lexi Nails is open Monday through Friday from 10 AM to 8 PM, Saturday from 9 AM to 6 PM, and Sunday from 11 AM to 5 PM. Walk-ins are welcome during all open hours, though calling ahead can shorten your wait, especially on weekends.

Where is Lexi Nails located, and is there parking?

Lexi Nails is located at 8362 Tamarack Village #115 in Woodbury, Minnesota, inside the Tamarack Village shopping center. The salon has a wheelchair accessible entrance and a wheelchair accessible parking lot, with free shopping-center parking right outside the door.

Does Lexi Nails take walk-ins?

Yes, Lexi Nails accepts walk-ins for manicures, pedicures, and gel services. You don't need an appointment, though calling ahead at (651) 738-9116 can help you avoid a wait, especially during busy Friday and Saturday afternoons.

What payment methods does Lexi Nails accept?

Lexi Nails accepts credit cards, debit cards, and NFC mobile payments like tap-to-pay. You can pay for your manicure, pedicure, or nail art service at the front desk however is most convenient for you, without needing cash on hand.

What nail services does Lexi Nails offer?

Lexi Nails offers gel and acrylic manicures, spa and luxury pedicures, dip powder, acrylic full sets and fills, gel polish and polish removal, and hand-painted nail art. Popular options include almond, coffin, and stiletto shapes, plus custom foil and glitter designs.

Is Lexi Nails wheelchair accessible?

Yes. Lexi Nails has a wheelchair accessible entrance, a wheelchair accessible parking lot, and a wheelchair accessible restroom, so guests using mobility devices can visit comfortably. The salon is on the ground floor of the Tamarack Village shopping center in Woodbury.
