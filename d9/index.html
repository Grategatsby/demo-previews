Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What are 2001 Nails of Woodbury's hours?

We're open Monday through Friday from 9:30 AM to 7:30 PM and Saturday from 9 AM to 6 PM. We're closed on Sunday. Appointments are recommended, especially on Saturdays.

Where are you located, and is parking easy?

We're at 1815 Radio Dr, Woodbury, MN 55125, with free lot parking right outside the door. Our entrance, parking lot, and restroom are all wheelchair accessible.

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are recommended to guarantee your preferred time, particularly on weekends. Call or text ahead and we'll find a slot that works for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and NFC mobile payments like Apple Pay and Google Pay, so you can pay however is easiest for you.

What nail services do you offer?

We offer manicures, pedicures, gel polish, dip powder, acrylic full sets, and hand-done nail art, all performed in a spacious, freshly updated salon.

Is the salon good for kids and families?

Yes. We welcome families, with dedicated kids' pedicure chairs and a staff experienced at first-time visits for children, so everyone can be pampered together.
