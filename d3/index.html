Dog Grooming FAQs in Oakdale

What are Sue's Clip Shop's hours?

Sue's Clip Shop doesn't publish set hours online. The fastest way to know if Sue can fit your dog in today is to call or text (651) 432-1158 and ask about same-day or upcoming availability.

Where is Sue's Clip Shop located?

Sue's Clip Shop is at 1967 Geneva Ave N, Oakdale, MN 55128. The shop has a wheelchair accessible parking lot on site, and you can get turn-by-turn directions from the Visit section below.

What payment methods does Sue's Clip Shop accept?

Sue's Clip Shop accepts both credit cards and debit cards, along with the usual in-shop payment options. Call ahead if you have a question about a specific payment method.

What services does Sue's Clip Shop offer?

Sue's Clip Shop offers full-service dog grooming, baths, nail trims, and breed-specific cuts. Sue works one dog (or one household's dogs) at a time rather than running an assembly line, so grooms are hands-on and personal.

Does Sue's Clip Shop serve the Oakdale area?

Yes. Sue's Clip Shop is based in Oakdale, Minnesota and has groomed local dogs since 1984, with many multi-generation households returning for over four decades.

Is Sue's Clip Shop wheelchair accessible?

Sue's Clip Shop has a wheelchair accessible parking lot on site. Call ahead if you have specific accessibility needs and Sue's team can help you plan your visit.
