Common Questions

Auto Repair FAQs for Conway & St Paul's East Side

Straight answers about hours, location, payments, and what Mike's team can help with.

What are Mike's Auto Repair's hours?

Mike's Auto Repair is open Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM and Saturday from 9 AM to 5 PM. The shop is closed on Sundays. Because it stays busy, appointments are recommended to guarantee a spot.

Where is Mike's Auto Repair located?

Mike's Auto Repair is located at 510 White Bear Ave in the Conway neighborhood on St Paul's East Side, Minnesota. The shop has a wheelchair accessible parking lot on site, making drop-off and pick-up easy.

What payment methods does Mike's Auto Repair accept?

Mike's Auto Repair accepts credit cards, debit cards, and NFC mobile payments such as Apple Pay and Google Pay, so you can pay however is most convenient when you pick up your vehicle.

Do I need an appointment at Mike's Auto Repair?

Yes, appointments are recommended at Mike's Auto Repair. The shop stays busy, so calling or texting (651) 735-1435 ahead of time helps guarantee your vehicle gets seen when you need it.

What services does Mike's Auto Repair offer?

Mike's Auto Repair is a general auto repair shop offering oil changes along with brake, engine, cooling, and other mechanical repairs, performed onsite by mechanics at their White Bear Ave shop.

Is Mike's Auto Repair wheelchair accessible?

Yes, Mike's Auto Repair has a wheelchair accessible parking lot on site, along with a restroom inside the shop for customers who are waiting on their vehicle.
